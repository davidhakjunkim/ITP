# Final Project Proposal

## Overview

Through this final project, I will create my own portfolio website.

### Resources

[STMPD RCRDS](https://stmpdrcrds.com/)

[OWSLA](https://owsla.com/)

[Skrillex](https://skrillex.com/)

Looking at portfolio websites such as those of artists and record labels, I found them truly impressive. because the artistic values ​​they aspire to are visually expressed through the website. Through this project, I aim not only to create a space where I can share my music with others, but also to express my artistic sensibilities within that space in my own unique way.

##### Will any work as part of this project overlap with any work you are doing outside of this class? For another class? Who is the professor in this class?

Yes, this project overlaps with my ongoing work as a musician and songwriter outside of this class, as I plan to include my projects in the portfolio. Through this project, I aim to improve my artistic vision and use the portfolio as a platform to showcase my identity and share my work with a wider audience.

## Outcomes

##### GOOD outcome for the final project.

A fully functional portfolio website with a basic layout, including an About page(myself) and a Projects section with at least two completed works.

##### Better outcome for the final project.

A clean and visually organized portfolio website with consistent design, basic styling (like typography and layout), and at least two to three projects presented clearly.

##### Best outcome for the final project.

A polished portfolio website with consistent design, basic responsiveness for different devices, smooth navigation, and a well-organized presentation of all projects, looks like professonal website that I used as resources.
